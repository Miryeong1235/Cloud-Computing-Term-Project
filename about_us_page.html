ReloCash is our term project for COMP 3962 - Cloud Computing Platforms at BCIT.

Our team—Darrel Giovanni Tapilaha, Ephraim Flores, and Misuzu Taniguchi—is excited to dive into cloud computing and create a practical financial management solution.

This project allows us to apply what we've learned in a real-world setting while developing a cloud-powered application that makes financial tracking more efficient and accessible.
